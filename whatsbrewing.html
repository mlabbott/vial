What's Brewing

A behind-the-scenes look at potioneering to help you on your way to mastery.

Brew a Forgetfulness Potion

This episode kicks off Belladonna's new series "Back to Basics." This series is meant for beginners, squibs, or those who have been out of the game for awhile and need a refresher course in potion making. The Forgetfulness Potion is an easy concoction for beginners, as it only has three ingredients and is relatively low maintenance.

Precautions with Potioneering

Episode two is all about the dangers of lazy potion brewing. As a potioneer, you have a responsibility to society to uphold a general sense of morality. Belladonna explains the messiness of playing with love, and the side-effects of indulging in too much liquid luck. These are basic dangers that every beginner should look out for.

Proper Cauldron Boil

This is what a standard pewter cauldron heated to exactly 450 degrees should sound like. If it is not bubbling as excitedly, you must raise the heat.
