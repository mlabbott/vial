Amortentia

Amortentia is the world's most powerful love potion. It does not have a distinctive scent, as it smells differently to each person and takes the form of what he/she finds most attractive. Please take caution when using a love potion; it does not create real love, only intense obsession, and therefore can be quite dangerous. Effects are not permanent.

Cost per vial: 17 sickles, 2 knuts
Cost per cauldron: 8 galleons, 1 sickle

Felix Felicis

Also known as "Liquid Luck", Felix Felicis makes the drinker successful at all they do for a limited amount of time. Beware, taking too much or too often can cause recklessness, overconfidence, and giddiness. Not for use in competitive sports.

Pepperup Potion

Pepperup Potion cures colds, headaches, and the flu. Common side-effects oftentimes include a funnel of steam coming out of one's ears. Not to worry, however, as that usually means it is doing its job.

Cost per vial: 2 sickles
Cost per cauldron: 5 galleons

Veritaserum

Veritaserum forces the drinker to tell the truth. While once used in wizarding courts, its use has been deemed inadmissable as it can be fooled by Occlumency. As it is a highly controlled substance by the Ministry of Magic, you must have a notorized letter to order it from The Venemous Vial. No exceptions.

Cost per vial: 10 sickles
Cost per cauldron: 5 galleons
