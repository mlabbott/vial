Sale of the Week

Polyjuice Potion allows the drinker to assume the form of someone else. Each batch has stewed for exactly one month, and all that is left for you to do is add the hair of the person you wish to transform into. Please note that the length of the effects vary per vial.

Cost per vial: 40 sickles 32 sickles
Cost per cauldron: 15 galleons 12 galleons

*Please note that we are not responsible for any side-effects that are caused by the type of hair you ingest. Polyjuice Potion is not recommended for use on animals or magical creatures.
